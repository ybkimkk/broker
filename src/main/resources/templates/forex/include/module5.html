Forex Pairs and Quotes

Currencies being traded are listed in pairs, such as USD/CAD, EUR/ USD, Or USD/JPYThese represent the U.S. dollar (USD) versus the Canadian dollar (CAD), the Euro(EUR) versus the USD, and the USD versus the Japanese Yen (JPY).
